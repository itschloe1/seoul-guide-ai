## Quick Answer

Olive Young is Korea's #1 beauty and health store — think Sephora meets drugstore, but with K-beauty. **9 out of 10 foreign tourists** shopping for cosmetics in Korea visit Olive Young. The best products to buy: **sunscreen** (Korean sunscreen is globally famous), **serums** (especially hyaluronic acid and snail mucin), and **sheet masks**. Look for the **Olive Young Awards stickers** (gold/silver) on shelves — those are verified best sellers. Foreign tourists get **tax refund** (VAT back) on purchases over 15,000 KRW.

## What Makes Korean Beauty Products Special

Why K-beauty products fly off the shelves:
- **Innovation cycle is faster** — Korean brands release new formulations every 3-6 months vs. 1-2 years for Western brands
- **Clinic-grade ingredients at drugstore prices** — PDRN, exosomes, retinol alternatives at ~10,000-30,000 KRW
- **Texture obsession** — Koreans won't buy a product if the texture feels bad, so everything feels premium
- **Layering culture** — Products designed to work together in multi-step routines

## Shopping Tips for Foreigners

### Tax Refund (세금 환급)

Purchases over **15,000 KRW** at a single Olive Young store qualify for VAT refund (~10% back).

**How:**
1. At checkout, show your passport
2. Store issues a tax refund receipt (or processes it automatically via the "Tax Refund" counter)
3. At the airport before departure, visit the tax refund kiosk or counter
4. Refund to your card or cash

**Flagship stores** (명동, 강남, 홍대) have dedicated tax refund counters inside the store — faster than airport.

### Olive Young Global (Online)

If you want to browse/buy before arriving in Korea or ship internationally:

- Good for researching products before your trip

## Common Questions

**Q: Is Olive Young cheaper in Korea than buying K-beauty abroad?**
A: Yes, significantly. Products are typically 30-50% cheaper in Korean Olive Young stores compared to global retailers, Amazon, or YesStyle. Plus you get the tax refund on top of that.

**Q: Which Olive Young products make the best souvenirs/gifts?**
A: Sheet mask multipacks (10-pack Mediheal ~10,000 KRW), rom&nd lip tints (~9,000 KRW), hand creams, and small skincare sets. The "1+1" (buy one get one) deals at Olive Young are perfect for gifts.

**Q: I have sensitive skin. What should I avoid?**
A: Avoid products with fragrance (향료) and alcohol (알코올) high in the ingredients list. Brands known for sensitive skin: SKIN1004, Round Lab Dokdo line, Torriden, Soon Jung (Etude). Ask staff: "민감성 피부용 있어요?" (Do you have products for sensitive skin?)

**Q: How do I know if a product is genuine?**
A: Buying at official Olive Young stores guarantees authenticity. Each product has a Korean FDA (식약처) approval number. Be cautious with third-party sellers online. Olive Young stores themselves are always authentic.

**Q: What does "1+1" mean on the price tag?**
A: Buy one, get one free. Olive Young frequently runs 1+1 promotions, especially on popular items. "2+1" means buy two, get one free. These are real discounts, not marketing tricks — take advantage of them.

**Q: Can I return products at Olive Young?**
A: Unopened products can be returned within 7 days with receipt. Opened products cannot be returned unless defective. Keep your receipt.

**Q: Do Olive Young staff speak English?**
A: In tourist areas (Myeongdong, Gangnam, Hongdae), many staff speak basic English. In neighborhood stores, limited English but they're helpful with gestures and phone translation apps. The Olive Young app has product information in English.

**Q: What's the best time to shop at Olive Young?**
A: Weekday mornings are least crowded. Major sale periods: CJ ONE sale (April, October), Olive Young Birthday Sale (June), Year-End Awards Sale (December-January). During sales, popular items sell out fast — go on day 1.

**Q: Can I buy Olive Young products at the airport?**
A: Yes. Incheon Airport has Olive Young stores in both departure terminals. Prices are similar to city stores, but selection is limited to best sellers. Better to shop in the city and claim tax refund at the airport.

**Q: I see "기획세트" on some products. What does that mean?**
A: "기획세트" means "planned set" — a special package deal with bonus items included (travel sizes, extra masks, tools). Same price as the main product alone but with extras. Always worth buying over the individual item.
