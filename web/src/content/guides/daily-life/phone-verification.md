## Quick Answer

Get a prepaid SIM or eSIM with a Korean +82 phone number as soon as you land. You only need your passport. Without a Korean phone number, you cannot use Kakao, Naver, delivery apps, banking, or most online services. If staying longer than 90 days, get your ARC first, then upgrade to a postpaid plan for cheaper rates and full identity verification access.

---

## Why This Matters

Your Korean phone number is your digital ID. Nearly every service requires one: KakaoTalk, banking, food delivery, e-commerce, transportation apps, and government services.

---

## Getting a Korean Phone Number

### At the Airport (Fastest)

All three major carriers have booths at Incheon Airport. Open 7am-10pm, English-speaking staff available. You only need your passport.

| Provider | 30-day Plan | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| KT | ~55,000 KRW | Includes Korean number |
| LG U+ | ~43,000-55,000 KRW | Includes Korean number |
| SK Telecom | ~71,000 KRW | Most expensive |

### eSIM Options

**Data-only eSIMs** (Airalo, Saily) are fine for internet but useless for Korean app verification -- they don't provide a Korean phone number.

**eSIMs with Korean number** (simcard.kr, Chingu Mobile) give you a real +82 number for calls, SMS, and app verification.

### Budget MVNOs

**Chingu Mobile** is the most foreigner-friendly MVNO. Plans from ~25,000 KRW/month with unlimited data. Accepts passport (no ARC needed for prepaid).

## Identity Verification Levels

| Level | What It Is | Requires ARC? |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| SMS Verification | 6-digit code via SMS | No |
| Carrier-based (통신사 본인인증) | Carrier checks your identity | Yes |
| PASS App | Biometric + carrier verification | Yes |
| Certificate-based (공동인증서) | Digital certificate from bank | Yes |

Without an ARC, you can only use Level 1 (SMS verification), which unlocks basic app registration but not banking or government services.

---

## After Getting Your ARC

1. Visit your carrier store with passport, ARC, and Korean bank card
2. Request ARC-based registration update
3. Keep your existing number using number portability (번호이동)
4. Set up autopay from your Korean bank account
5. Install the PASS app for full identity verification

---

## Common Problems

- **SMS code doesn't arrive:** Check number format, wait 2-3 minutes, restart phone
- **PASS app fails:** Your carrier probably still has you registered with passport instead of ARC
- **App language is Korean only:** Set your phone's system language to English before opening the app
