## Quick Answer

Get a **T-money card** at any convenience store (2,500 won), charge it with cash, and you can ride every subway, bus, and most taxis in the country. Download **Kakao Map** or **Naver Map** for English navigation. For taxis, install **K-RIDE** (no Korean phone needed) or **Kakao T**. Seoul's public transit is clean, safe, affordable, and runs until around midnight.

**Minimum budget for a day of transit in Seoul:** 5,000-8,000 won (roughly $4-6 USD).

---

## T-money Card

T-money is a rechargeable transit card that works on subways, buses, taxis, convenience stores, and vending machines nationwide.

- **Where to buy:** Convenience stores (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven), subway station machines
- **Cost:** 2,500-4,000 won (no ID required)
- **How to charge:** Cash at convenience stores or subway machines
- **Recommended balance:** 30,000-50,000 won for 3-4 days in Seoul

## Bus

Seoul buses are color-coded: Blue (trunk routes), Green (feeders to subway), Yellow (downtown loops), Red (express to satellite cities). **Cash is not accepted** on buses -- you must use a transit card.

Free transfers between buses and subway up to 5 times within 30 minutes.

## Taxi

### Fares (Seoul, 2025)

| Type | Base Fare |
|------|-----------|
| Regular (orange/silver) | 4,800 won (1.6 km) |
| Deluxe (black) | 7,000 won (3.0 km) |

Late-night surcharges apply from 10 PM to 4 AM on regular taxis.

### K-RIDE (Best for Tourists)
No Korean phone or Kakao account needed. English UI with auto-translation chat for drivers. International credit cards accepted. 20-30% more expensive than Kakao T.

### Kakao T
The dominant taxi app. Can register with a foreign phone number. English UI available.

## Common Questions

**Q: Is Google Maps useful in Korea?**
For walking, somewhat. For transit routing and driving, no. Use **Kakao Map** or **Naver Map** instead.

**Q: How late does public transit run?**
Subways: last trains around 11:30 PM - midnight. Night buses (N-routes) run midnight to 5 AM.

**Q: Can I get a T-money card at the airport at 2 AM?**
Yes. Convenience stores in Incheon Airport arrivals operate 24 hours.
