## Quick Answer

Korea has the most advanced food delivery culture on earth. Over 80% of Koreans order via app at least once a week, and deliveries arrive in 15-30 minutes. For foreigners:

- **Coupang Eats** is the easiest for residents (English UI, fast delivery)
- **Shuttle Delivery** is fully English-native and takes international cards (no Korean phone needed)
- **Baemin** (2026) added AI-powered English support and foreign credit card acceptance

You do not need to tip. You do not need to speak Korean. You can get food delivered to parks, hotel lobbies, and subway station entrances.

## For Tourists (No Korean Phone or Card)

**Shuttle Delivery** is your best option. No Korean phone, no ARC, accepts international cards and PayPal. Also available in Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Japanese, and Chinese. Coverage is limited to select Seoul districts, Pyeongtaek, Busan, and Daegu.

**Baemin** also allows guest ordering with foreign phone numbers and cards as of 2026.

---

## For Residents (With ARC)

**Coupang Eats** is the recommended choice. Native English interface, fastest delivery (15-20 minutes average). With Coupang Wow membership (4,990 won/month), you get free delivery.

## Delivery Tips

- **Door codes:** Add your building entrance code in delivery notes or the driver will call you in Korean
- **Tipping:** Not expected in Korea. No tipping function in any delivery app
- **Contactless:** Default for most orders. Driver leaves food at your door and sends a photo
