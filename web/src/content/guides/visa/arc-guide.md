## Quick Answer

Every foreigner staying in South Korea longer than 90 days must apply for a **Residence Card** (formerly called ARC). Apply **within 90 days of arrival** or face fines up to 1,000,000 KRW. The card costs **30,000 KRW** and processing takes **3-6 weeks**. As of January 2025, a **Mobile Residence Card** with the same legal validity is also available.

---

## Who Needs an ARC?

Anyone on a long-term visa (D, E, F, H series) staying more than 90 days. Common visa types: D-2 (Student), E-2 (English Teacher), E-7 (Skilled Worker), F-6 (Marriage), H-1 (Working Holiday).

**You do NOT need one** if you're a tourist (B-1/B-2) staying under 90 days.

---

## How to Book an Appointment

Book at [hikorea.go.kr](https://www.hikorea.go.kr) immediately after arriving. Select "Foreign Resident Registration." Wait time: 1-2 weeks normally, 3-4 weeks during peak periods.

**Tip:** Book the day you arrive. Check daily for cancellation slots.

---

## Required Documents

| Document | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Passport + photocopy | ID/visa page |
| Application form | Available at office or hikorea.go.kr |
| Color photo | 3.5 x 4.5 cm, white background, ears visible |
| Proof of residence | Lease, dorm certificate, or host confirmation |
| Fee: 30,000 KRW cash | Additional ~3,000-4,000 KRW for home delivery |

Additional documents vary by visa type (employment certificate, medical exam, enrollment certificate, etc.).

---

## At the Immigration Office

1. Arrive 10-15 minutes early
2. Pay fee at ATM (English interface available)
3. Submit all documents when your number is called
4. Choose office pickup or home delivery
5. Receive Certificate of Application (proof of legal status while waiting)

---

## Processing Time

| Situation | Wait Time |
|-----------|-----------|
| Standard | 3-4 weeks |
| Peak season (Feb-Mar, Aug-Sep) | 6-10 weeks |
| Lost card replacement | 3-4 days |

**Do not leave Korea during processing** -- it may cancel your application.

---

## What ARC Unlocks

| With ARC | Without ARC |
|----------|-------------|
| Full bank account | Basic tourist account only |
| Postpaid phone plan | Tourist SIM only |
| Health insurance enrollment | Full out-of-pocket costs |
| Online services (Coupang, Baemin, etc.) | Cannot verify identity |
| Official housing lease | Difficult to sign |

---

## Common Problems

- **No appointment slots:** Check every morning, try different offices, try different booth numbers
- **Photo rejected:** Ears must be fully visible, white background, 3.5 x 4.5 cm
- **Name mismatch:** All documents must match passport exactly
- **Card lost:** Report within 14 days, replacement costs 30,000 KRW (3-4 days)
- **Address change:** Update within 14 days at your community center (free)
