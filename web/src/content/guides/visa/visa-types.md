## Quick Answer

Korea has dozens of visa types, but most foreigners fall into about 10 categories. The most common confusion: which visa lets you do what, how to switch between them, and what happens if you miss a deadline. When in doubt, call **1345** (foreigner helpline).

---

## Most Common Visa Types

### Work Visas

| Visa | Name | For Who | Duration |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| E-2 | Teaching | English teachers | 1 year (renewable) |
| E-7 | Skilled Worker | IT, engineering, design professionals | 1-3 years |
| E-9 | Non-professional | Factory, agriculture workers | 3 years |

### Student Visas

| Visa | For Who | Work Allowed? |
|------|---------|--------------|
| D-2 | University degree programs | Part-time, 20hrs/week with permit |
| D-4 | Korean language school | Limited, after 6 months |

### Residence Visas

| Visa | For Who | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| F-1-D | Digital nomads (new 2025) | 1-2 years |
| F-2 | Points-based residence | 3 years |
| F-5 | Permanent residence | Unlimited |
| F-6 | Marriage to Korean national | 2 years |

### Other

| Visa | Notes |
|------|-------|
| H-1 Working Holiday | Age 18-30, 1 year |
| D-10 Job Seeker | 6 months-2 years |
| B-1/B-2 Tourist | 90 days, NO work |

---

## Digital Nomad Visa (F-1-D) -- New in 2025

Requirements: employed by a company outside Korea, annual income $65,000+ USD, health insurance, no criminal record. You can live in Korea and work remotely but **cannot** work for Korean companies or freelance for Korean clients. Duration: 1 year, renewable once.

---

## Critical Deadlines

### Address Reporting
**Report new address within 14 days of moving.** Fine up to 200,000 KRW for late reporting.

### Visa Renewal
**Apply at least 30 days before expiry.** Fee: 60,000 KRW. Book at hikorea.go.kr.

### Overstay Consequences

| Duration | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|
| 1-30 days | Fine 200,000-500,000 KRW |
| 30 days - 1 year | Fine + possible deportation + 1-year re-entry ban |
| 1+ years | Fine + deportation + 3-year ban |
| Working illegally | Fine + deportation + 5-year ban |

## Switching Visa Types

| From | To | Possible? |
|------|----|-----------|
| D-2 Student | E-7 Skilled Worker | Yes, need job offer |
| D-2 Student | D-10 Job Seeker | Yes, after graduation |
| E-2 Teacher | E-7 Skilled Worker | Yes |
| Tourist B-1 | Any work visa | No, must leave Korea |

**Apply before your current visa expires.** Fee: 100,000 KRW. Processing: 2-4 weeks.
